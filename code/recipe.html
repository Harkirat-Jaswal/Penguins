Poutine Recpie

Information About Dish

Poutine is a wonderful and delicious concoction of fries, gravy and cheese curds and is one of the most popular Canadian dishes. This recipe gives a serving size of 1.5.

Ingredients

Poutine Gravy

  • 1.5 Tbsp Cornstrarch
  • 1 Tbsp Water
  • 3 Tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 0.13 Cup Unbleached All Purpose Flour
  • 10 oz Beef Broth
  • 5 oz Chicken Broth
  • Pepper, To Taste

Deep Fried Fries

  • 1 lbs Russet Potatoes, (3-4 Medium Potatoes)
  • Peanut or Other Frying Oil

Toppings

  • 0.5 - 0.75 Cups White Cheddar Cheese Curds

Instructions

    Poutine Gravy

    • In a small bowl, dissolve the cornstarch in the water and set aside.

    • In a large saucepan, melt the butter. Add the flour and cook, stirring regularly, for about 5 minutes, until the mixture turns golden brown.

    • Add the beef and chicken broth and bring to a boil. Stir in about half the cornstarch mixture and simmer for a minute.

    • Season with pepper. Taste and add additional salt, if necessary. Make ahead and re-warm or keep warm until your fries are ready.

    Deep Fried Fries

    • Prepare your potatoes and cut into 1/2-inch thick sticks. Place into a large bowl and cover completely with cold water.

    • When ready to cook, heat your oil in your deep fryer or large, wide, heavy cooking pot to 300° F.

    • Remove the potatoes from the water and place onto a sheet of paper towel. Blot to remove as much excess moisture as possible.

    • Add your fries to the 300°F oil and cook for 5-8 minutes. Remove potatoes from oil and scatter on a wire rack.

    • Increase oil temperature to 375°F Once oil is heated to that temperature.

    • Return the potatoes to the fryer and cook until potatoes are golden brown. Remove to a paper towel-lined bowl.

    Preparing Poutine

    • Add your fried or baked fries to a large, clean bowl. Season lightly with salt while still warm.

    • Add a ladle of hot poutine gravy to the bowl and using tongs, toss the fries in the gravy.

    • Add the cheese curds and toss with the hot fries and gravy. Serve with freshly ground pepper. Serve immediately.
