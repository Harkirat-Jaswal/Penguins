All About Monkeys

Monkeys are the only primates with tails and are mammals of the inraorder Simiiformes. Monkeys live together in social groups. All members contribute by helping to defend food sources, raise young, and watch for predators. Monkeys have evolved many ways of communicating, including visual cues, auditory calls.

Monkeys eating fruit from trees, most commonly bananas. However, since many also eat some form of animal protein, they are generally omnivores, not herbivores. Monkeys are omnivores that eat a varied diet that can include nuts, fruits, seeds, eggs, insects, lizards, and more.
